Why Canada?

Canada is one of the most multicultural countries in the world, with clean and friendly cities, world-class universities, and business and job opportunities. Canada has led all G-7 countries in economic growth from 2003-2012, and has the 2nd highest standard of living in the G-20 (measured by GDP per capita).

Canada is a diverse and multicultural country with plenty of support to help you and your family achieve success when you arrive in Canada. Freedom is the foundation of Canadian life. We are a peaceful nation with low crime rates. We promote equal opportunity for all, in all areas of life.

About Us?
